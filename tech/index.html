Warm welcoming atmosphere

Aiming to offer you an experience of a lifetime, Divine Cave Experience has thought of its offerings down to the last detail. Making a good
first impression from the start, the lobby area has been designed to ensure comfort and create a warm welcoming atmosphere for all
guests to enjoy while the small side patio invites you to relax while we are taking care of your check-in, luggage, or transportation. The
hotel also features storage areas for guests' luggage and other items as well as a common staircase leading you to the hotel's outdoor areas.


A Divine Wellness Experience

Secluded in an old wine cave of 100 square meters, still carrying the fruity aromas and subtle notes of oak barrels, the Wellness Cave Area
invites you on a journey of rejuvenation and relaxation. Only available for private use, the Wellness Cave Area is a divine retreat ready to
captivate your senses. Enjoy the ultimate wellness VIP treatment as you immerse in Santorini's natural volcanic waters in the 50 square
meter pool within the illuminated rocky curves creating the feeling of a dream you are having with your eyes open wide.


Divine Flavours and Aromas

A lucious BREAKFAST: Esteemed guests staying at the Divine are welcome to wake to the fresh scents of the morning and enjoy a
luscious Greek breakfast at their designated rooftop cabana on the roof terrace in view of the azure Aegean sea. Fresh squeezed orange
juice and eclectic locally produced treats will add flavor to their every morning.

Divine POOL BAR: Located on the rooftop by the pool is the ideal lounge area for the hot summer days. Get ready to relax and
unwind with signature cocktails and nutricius juices from our experienced mixologists or taste from a selection of fresh salads,
light dishes and desserts.

Ylia Restaurant: With mesmerizing views to caldera & sunset, situated at the top of the Divine Rooftop terrace with mesmerizing
views to Caldera, sunset and Oia, Ylia Restaurant promises to take its guests on a succulent journey to the Mediterranean gastronomy.
The A la cart Menu is inspired by the Cycladic culinary tradition infused with flavors and aromas from all over Greece.
Dream away with chill out music and gaze in awe at the incredible vistas of the Aegean sea, the imposing caldera and Santorini's dramatic scenery.
Opening hours: 18.00-23.00
